## COMPARATIVE SUMMARY

### Ad Volume (Active Ads in Nigeria)
- Samsung: ~2,100-2,300 (highest volume)
- Xiaomi/Redmi: ~480-560
- Tecno: ~120-410

### Brand Presence Type
- Xiaomi/Redmi: Official brand page (Xiaomi Nigeria) running direct campaigns
- Samsung: No official brand page; entirely retailer/distributor ecosystem
- Tecno: Minimal official brand presence; primarily retailer-driven

### Primary Campaign Focus
- Xiaomi/Redmi: Single product launch (Redmi Note 15 Series) - feature-focused
- Samsung: Multi-product, multi-tier (budget A-series + premium S-series + foldables)
- Tecno: Full range from budget to flagship, camera-first positioning

### Key Differentiators in Messaging
- Xiaomi/Redmi: Durability ("Titan Durability"), gaming, water resistance, value offers
- Samsung: Luxury/premium (foldables), seasonal deals, retailer trust, warranty
- Tecno: Everyday hustle, camera quality, affordability, BNPL financing

### CTA Strategy
- Xiaomi/Redmi: Shop Now → mi.com (direct e-commerce)
- Samsung: Shop Now → retailer websites + WhatsApp
- Tecno: WhatsApp DM + website + Instagram

### Pricing Transparency
- All three brands: Explicit pricing in retailer ads
- Tecno: Most transparent with full price lists
- Samsung: Mix of promotional pricing and MSRP

### Platform Distribution
- All three: Facebook + Instagram primary
- Tecno/Samsung: Heavy WhatsApp integration
- Xiaomi: Facebook + Instagram focused
